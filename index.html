Where the work usually starts

Most projects come in through one of four needs.

Panel & service upgrades

Capacity planning, cleaner organization, and room for what the property needs next.

EV charging

Dedicated-circuit planning, clean routing, and charger placement that works every day.

Generator readiness

Transfer strategy, critical-load planning, and safer backup power preparation.

Troubleshooting

Root-cause diagnosis for nuisance trips, dead circuits, unreliable devices, and aging systems.

What a premium install should feel like

Good electrical work solves the problem. Great electrical work also improves the experience.

1

Scope first

Clarify the actual issue, the target outcome, and any finish or access constraints before the work begins.

2

Route intentionally

Choose circuit paths, equipment placement, and device locations that make sense both now and later.

3

Finish cleanly

Label panels clearly, keep the work area controlled, and leave the system easier to understand than before.

4

Plan ahead

Leave room for future loads, service calls, remodel phases, and maintenance instead of creating bottlenecks.
