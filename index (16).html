Surge protection

Whole-home surge protection: what it does

Whole-home surge protection helps reduce damage from electrical spikes and is often a simple, high-confidence upgrade.

  • Helps with many transient spikes entering the system.
  • Pairs well with panel work and modernization.
  • Adds protection for appliances and electronics.
  • Does not replace good grounding or fix overloaded circuits.

Quick takeaway

It is easy to ignore until the day you wish it was already there.

What it helps with

It can divert many common surge events before they reach branch circuits and equipment.

What it does not replace

It will not fix wiring defects, overloads, or every utility issue.

When it makes sense

Often during panel work or when planning for long-term reliability.
